# Xinux haqida

Xinux eng boshida **Genemator (hozirgi [Yuri](https://github.com/katsuki-yuri))** tomonidan yaratilgan hamjamiyat.
Boshida jamiyatning asosiy maqsadi O'zbek segmentida Linux jamiyatini rivojlantirish va hamda Linux uchun o'z hissamizni
qo'shish bo'lgan. Keyinchalik jamiyat kattarishi bilan uning maqsad va ambitsiyalari kattarib boraverdi. Jamiyat
2021 yilda Uzinfocom tomonidan o'z qanotiga olindi va Uzinfocom ostida "Xinux" nomli shaxsiy lokalizatsion Linux distributiv ishlab
chiqish yo'lga qo'yildi.

## Asosiy jamoa

Xinux jamiyatini boshqaruvchi asosiy jamoa mavjud va ularga Xinux asoschisi [Yuri](https://github.com/katsuki-yuri)
kiradi. So'ngla unga [Ismoilovdev](https://github.com/ismoilovdevml) va [Yetimdasturchi](https://github.com/yetimdasturchi)
qo'shildi. To'liq Xinux ga o'z hissalarini qo'shgan dasturchilarni [GitHub](https://github.com/uzinfocom-org/xinux/graphs/contributors)
da topishingiz mumkin.

## Tashakkurlar

Xinux jamiyati avvalambor ushbu guruh yoki dasturchilar uchun yaratgan ajoyib texnologiyalari uchun o'z minnatdorchiligini
bildiradi:

- https://github.com/torvalds/linux
- https://github.com/archlinux
- https://github.com/GNOME

- https://github.com/systemd
- https://github.com/pulseaudio

- https://github.com/vercel
- https://github.com/nginx
